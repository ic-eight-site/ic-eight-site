How it works

Workflow

From context reading to delivery, each step is handled with intent and tone awareness.

  1. 1. Intake & Context Reading
    You share your English content. We look at purpose, audience, and where it lives — blog, website, or in-product.
  2. 2. Tone & Intent Analysis
    We decode the brand voice and emotional tone before any translation happens.
  3. 3. Adaptation & Rewriting
    We rebuild meaning naturally in Japanese — adjusting rhythm, hierarchy, and nuance.
  4. 4. Refinement & QA
    We review consistency, punctuation, and readability. AI tools may assist, but judgment stays human.
  5. 5. Delivery & Feedback
    Clean, final copy ready for use — and a short feedback loop if you want revisions.
