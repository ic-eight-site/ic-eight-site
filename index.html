Why We Exist

I spent over 25 years as a translator — and hated every moment I wasn’t allowed to ask:

  • “Why does this need to be translated?”
  • “Will this make users act?”

Translation assumes the source is right. But most of the time, the source is the problem.

So I stopped translating. Now I help teams fix the Japanese layer of their product — before it ever reaches a translator.

What We Do

We help global SaaS and AI products work in Japan by designing UX-native Japanese — including:

  • Messaging that reflects your brand in context
  • Help and UI copy that users don’t have to “read twice”
